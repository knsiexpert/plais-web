Greetings. Thank you for your membership in AIS. We value your participation in our global community of IS academics, researchers, and practitioners.

As you may be aware, AIS's membership dues rates are based on the United Nation's Human Development Index (HDI), with differing rates for countries categorized in the four HDI tiers. The UN periodically revises its assessment of the countries included, which can shift countries into higher or lower tiers. This in turn impacts the dues rates for AIS members in those countries.

Poland experienced such a shift recently, moving from the UN's High to Very High HDI. This changed the dues rate for academic members in Poland from $70 USD to $175 USD. At the urging of Stanislaw Wrycza, president of the Polish Chapter of AIS (PLAIS), AIS reconsidered our practice of automatically increasing dues to match the HDI categorization. The AIS Council approved a temporary discount to help ease members into the new dues tier.

Thanks to Dr. Wrycza's leadership on this issue, AIS members in Poland will receive this discount for two years - through June 2016. During this time the temporary dues rate for members in Poland will be $123 (academic members) and $84 (doctoral student members), which is the mid-point between the High and Very High dues rates. When you login to your membership record, you will see the new discounted rate on the billing page of the renewal form.

We hope this discount will encourage your continued involvement in AIS for years to come. Please let me know if you have any questions.
