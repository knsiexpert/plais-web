The newly elected officers of the IS Acadmic Heads International
(ISAHI) have taken office following their online election. With
congratulations, the newly elected ISAHI officers are

President Anne P. Massey, Indianna University
Vice President Stanislaw Wrycza, University of Gdansk
Secretary Jasbir Dhaliwal, University of Memphis

As the outgoing president, I am delighted to see the organizational
leadership pass into such competent hands.

ISAHI is an organization of administrative heads responsible for
university level colleges, schools, institutes and departments for
Information Systems (Department Chairs, Academic Heads, Heads of
School, Deans, Directors and Conveners) that seeks to facilitate
sharing of information and resources, to sponsor relevant development
activities, and to promote professional management of these
organizations; and other activities deemed appropriate by the members.


// Richard Baskerville, CIS Dept., Georgia State University
