Letter from Claudia Loebbecke, The AIS President.


From: "Claudia Loebbecke" <claudia.loebbecke@uni-koeln.de>
Cc: "'Stanislaw Wrycza'" <swrycza@univ.gda.pl>
Sent: Wednesday, January 25, 2006 9:54 PM
Subject: [isworld] Polish AIS Chapter (PLAIS) unanimously approved

Dear Polish AIS friends,
Dear AISWorld community,

    I am very happy to inform you that AIS Council unanimously approved the establishment of a Polish Chapter of AIS (PLAIS).

    On behalf of AIS Council and the AIS community, I would like to thank Professor Stanislaw Wrycza for his initiative, his efforts, and his patience.

    Now it is time to get started; we look forward to working with you!

Congratulations and kind regards - Claudia

---
Professor Dr. Claudia Loebbecke, M.B.A.
